The Practice

Healing held in warm, attentive hands.

Hi, I'm Laylou — a bodywork practitioner working across Coquitlam and the surrounding Tri-Cities. My work lives at the meeting point of structure and energy: the precise release of deep tissue, the gentle listening of cranio sacral therapy, and the quiet recalibration of Reiki.

Every body arrives with its own story. Rather than following a script, I read what's in front of me and respond — adjusting pressure, pace and technique session by session so the work meets you exactly where you are.

Because I come to you, there's no waiting room, no rushing off afterward. Just a calm space made in your own home, and the time to actually settle into it.

Modalities

Four ways to come back to your body.

01

Cranio Sacral.

A light-touch therapy that follows the subtle rhythm of the craniosacral system. Deeply calming, it helps quiet the nervous system, ease tension headaches, and restore a sense of regulation and flow.

02

Deep Tissue.

Focused, sustained pressure that works into the deeper layers of muscle and fascia. Ideal for chronic tightness, knotted shoulders, and the holding patterns that build up from stress and repetitive strain.

03

Swedish.

The classic, flowing massage of long gliding strokes and gentle kneading. It boosts circulation, melts everyday tension, and offers the kind of full-body relaxation that leaves you genuinely lighter.

04

Reiki.

An energy practice working with the body's natural balance. Through light or hovering hands, Reiki invites deep rest, eases emotional heaviness, and creates space for the body's own healing to take the lead.
